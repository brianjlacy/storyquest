Welcome to StoryQuest Haven

It is a book, but much more than a book.

Neal Stephenson, The Diamond Age

With StoryQuest, you can create rich, interactive books for a variety of platforms. Using StoryQuest Haven, you can create your book, add moving and sounding content, create storylines and let others read your creations. You can then download your book for phones, tablets or eReaders or you can sell your books right from StoryQuest.

For getting started, read the documentation or browse some example books from our catalog. Also, take a look at the community forums to let people help you out. You can also take one of our tours thru the Haven, which explain creating and writing books using StoryQuest.

You can publish StoryQuest books to any or all of the stores and devices below:

If you want to publish for ePub eReaders or Amazon Kindle devices, please note that these platforms have limited multimedia capablilities. Refer to the manual to understand which features of StoryQuest are compatible with those platforms.
